Description

Do you like hiking? Do you want to read fun, humorous and fantastic facts? If you answer YES, then it’s time to grab this book and get ready for lots of fun facts about the UK! The UK is the country of beautiful people with lots of special cultures, historical artifacts, and incredible natural landmarks. No wonder, it has become one of the top tourist destinations in the world, despite not being the cheapest spot to fly at. Words can not explain the stunning views of the coast, the magnificent parks, the splendor of the streets and the gastronomic smells of the ancient cities and towns in the UK. That’s exactly why I suggest packing your stuff and going to visit the UK at least once in your life.

 

But no matter whether you’re only dreaming of visiting the UK or living there, this exclusive series of family-friendly fun facts will certainly teach you many new things. It’s a perfect book for night puzzle games, quizzes, questions and answers, and whether or not games believe. It can be particularly fun to use in waiting rooms, on the beach, on holidays, on long journeys by car, train and airplane, and in places like a public toilet. Every reader, even the boy, will enjoy this.
